Network of Super Smash Bros. Ultimate Franchise Links

By Will Schuckenbrock and Jack Schwanewede

This visualization depicts crossovers between Super Smash Bros. Ultimate (2018) and several other video game franchises. Different video game franchises are represented by nodes (circles), and a crossover is represented by the links (lines). The nodes are colored based on whether they are a Nintendo Franchise, where red represents Nintendo and blue represents a third party developer. The size of the nodes are also dependent on the amount of copies sold. The link color depends on what type of crossover the games have. Pink means the franchise has a playable Fighter in Smash, Green is a different crossver with Smash (Item, Stage, Spirit, etc.), and gray are crossovers between franchises. Highlighting each node reveals more information about the franchise, such as the name, copies sold, genre, and number of crossovers.
